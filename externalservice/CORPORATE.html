Issuance of Community Tax Certificate - Corporate

The Community Tax Certificate (CTC) or a Cedula is a document issued by the Philippine government to individuals and corporations upon payment of the community tax.

Office or Division: Business Taxes & Fees Division
Classification: Simple
Type of Transaction: Government to Business
Who may avail: All Corporate Business Establishments in Mandaue City
No. Client Steps Agency Actions Fees to be Paid Processing Time Person Responsible
1 Submit the Tax Order of Payment to Counter 9 Encode the details of the client and compute the amount to be paid None 3 minutes Revenue Collectors
2 Pay the required amount Issue the Community Tax Certificate Annual basic community tax of PHP 500 plus PHP 2 for every PHP 1,000 of gross receipts, up to PHP 10,000 5 minutes Revenue Collectors
