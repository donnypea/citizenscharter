Issuance of Certification Related to Business License and Fees

Certification of Last Payment is required in renewal of business license; Certification of No Business for residents requesting SSS Claims and other purposes; Certification of Retirement required by BIR for closing of business and verification.

Office or Division: Business Taxes & Fees Division
Classification Simple
Type of Transaction Government to Business
Who may avail Owners of business establishments in Mandaue City

Checklist of Requirements

Requirement Where to Secure
Request Form City Treasurer’s Office – Business Taxes & Fees Division
Certification of No Business – request from the BIR City Treasurer’s Office – Business Taxes & Fees Division
Certification of Retirement – Approved retirement application City Treasurer’s Office – Business Taxes & Fees Division
