Receipt of Payment of Business Taxes (New & Renewal)

Business establishments are required to pay business taxes and other regulatory fees. Business permits must be renewed every year and penalties are imposed on business establishments that fail to renew during the prescribed period. Taxes may be paid in an annual or quarterly basis.

Office or Division Business Taxes & Fees Division
Classification Simple
Type of Transaction Government to Business
Who may avail Owners of business establishments in Mandaue City

Tax Order of Payment (2 copies) Business Permit Licensing Office (BPLO) or through Electronic Business Permit Licensing System (eBPLS)
