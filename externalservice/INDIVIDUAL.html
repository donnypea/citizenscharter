Issuance of Community Tax Certificate – Individual

The Community Tax Certificate (CTC) or Cedula is issued by the Philippine government to individuals and corporations upon payment of the community tax. It is also used in various government transactions.

Office or Division Business Taxes & Fees Division
Classification Simple
Type of Transaction Government to Citizen, Government to Business, Government to Government
Who may avail All Filipino Citizens

Checklist of Requirements

Requirement Where to Secure
Filled-out application form City Treasurer’s Office – Business Taxes & Fees Division Lobby
