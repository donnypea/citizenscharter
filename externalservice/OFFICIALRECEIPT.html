Request for Certified True Copy of Official Receipt

The service aims to accommodate the request of any individual to obtain a certified true copy of Official Receipts and Tax Order of Payment - as reference for payment of taxes, Community Tax Certificates and payment of fees and charges for other purposes it may serve.
